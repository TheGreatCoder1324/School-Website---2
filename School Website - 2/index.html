Welcome to DPS Patna

Making Education More Fun.

Takshila Educational Society laid the foundation of veritable learning in Patna by establishing Delhi Public School in the year 1998 in a collaborative effort with the Delhi Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. DPS Patna is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII.

Campus Life


Multipurpose Room

Was established in 1978

DPS Patna School has a multipurpose room. This room is used to pick up lunch, dance, play games, have parties (rarely), listen to announcments, and more. Without this room, the school wouldn't be half as fun as it is now. This room has many uses and it is really helpful.

school library

Was built in 1980

This is a library where students can enjoy reading books. The library is filled with books and stories kids can read. Kids also have a comfortable place for sitting and reading their books. This library is one of the best places in the school.

School Field

Was built in 1982

The school field is for kids to play sports. It is mainly used for Physical Education. Sometimes, it is used to play football, soccer, and other sports. Kids also use it for the running competition in the school.
